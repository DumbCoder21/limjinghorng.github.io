Hello Fresh Campaign

Aims

The campaign aims and underscores the concerning dietary habits of young people, emphasizing their high consumption of fast food and soft drinks, coupled with a low adherence to national standards for fruit and vegetable intake. It highlights the common perception among young adults that a lack of time is a significant obstacle to maintaining a healthy diet, leading to time-deepening behaviors such as cutting back on cooking and eating time. To tackle these challenges, introducing HelloFresh, a service designed to help individuals, like the reader, create healthy meal plans with minimal time and effort. HelloFresh addresses the difficulty of adhering to strict dietary rules and recognizes common reasons for neglecting one's diet, including a busy schedule, laziness, lack of cooking skills, and a preference for takeout.

The target audience encompasses individuals of all age groups who aspire to maintain a healthy diet but encounter obstacles mentioned above. With a notable portion of the adult population in Malaysia at risk for chronic diseases. Therefore, to tackle the situation and raising awareness we decided to use social media as HelloFresh has a decent social media presence. It has its presence on almost all social media platforms, regularly updating its followers with all the happenings around the brand. It constantly tries to reply to all the comments left by its customers and users on Twitter and Facebook so that customers can feel like their compliments, complaints, and concerns are addressed and directed to customer service if required.
